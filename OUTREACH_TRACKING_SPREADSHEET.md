## üí° PRO TIPS

**Tip 1: Use Gmail Labels**
Create labels: "Defensible Hiring - Sent", "DH - Replied", "DH - Demo Booked", "DH - Proposal"
Move emails through pipeline visually.

**Tip 2: Set Up Canned Responses**
Gmail Settings ‚Üí Advanced ‚Üí Enable "Canned Responses"
Save your most common replies as templates for speed.

**Tip 3: LinkedIn Connection First**
Before cold email, send LinkedIn connection request: "Hi [Name], I'm researching AI hiring compliance for NYC companies like [Company]. Would love to connect."
48 hours later ‚Üí Send cold email (now they recognize your name).

**Tip 4: Track Competitor Intel**
Add column: "What solution are they using now?"
If 5+ companies say "We use [Competitor]", you know your competitive landscape.

**Tip 5: Ask for Referrals**
After closing customer #1, immediately ask: "Do you know 2 other HR leaders facing the same compliance challenge?"
Referrals close 3X faster than cold outreach.
