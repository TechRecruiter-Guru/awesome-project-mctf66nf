## üí° Pro Tips

### **Personalization at Scale:**
1. **Reference their "Why Selected" reason** in first line
2. **Use company-specific pain points** (EU AI Act for EU companies, Series B for late-stage)
3. **Mention their robot by name** (Apollo, Digit, Phoenix, etc.)
4. **Tie to recent news** (funding announcement, partnership, product launch)

### **Spam Avoidance:**
- Send max 50 emails/day from one address
- Use 2+ email addresses if sending 100 (Day 1: 35, Day 2: 35, Day 3: 30)
- Avoid spam trigger words: "free," "guarantee," "limited time" (unless genuine)
- Always include unsubscribe option
- Warm up domain: send 10 emails to yourself/friends first

### **Response Handling:**
**If they reply positively:**
- Respond within 1 hour
- Send Calendly immediately
- Prepare custom demo (use their vertical template)

**If they reply "not interested":**
- Thank them
- Ask if they know anyone who might benefit
- Add to "nurture" list for 6-month check-in

**If they ask "how does it work?"**
- Send 2-min Loom video demo
- Offer live demo
- Send sample safety case HTML
