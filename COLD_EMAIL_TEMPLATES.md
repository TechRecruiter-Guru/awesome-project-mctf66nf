## üéØ EMAIL SENDING BEST PRACTICES

### Timing
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best times:** 10 AM - 11 AM or 2 PM - 3 PM (recipient's timezone)
- **Avoid:** Mondays (inbox overload), Fridays (mentally checked out)

### Personalization Checklist
Before hitting send, customize:
- [ ] Replace `[Company]` with actual company name
- [ ] Replace `[First Name]` with decision maker's first name
- [ ] Add one personalized sentence (recent news, mutual connection, specific pain point)
- [ ] Confirm company location matches your compliance urgency claim (NYC, CA, IL)
- [ ] Verify you're using correct email address (test with Hunter.io)

### Tracking & Follow-Up
- Use email tracking (Mailtrack Chrome extension, free)
- If opened but no reply after 3 days ‚Üí Send Follow-Up #1
- If opened 2+ times but no reply ‚Üí They're interested but need nudge ‚Üí Call it out: "I saw you opened this twice‚Äîwant to chat?"
- If NOT opened after 3 days ‚Üí Try different subject line

### A/B Testing
Send 5 emails with Subject A, 5 emails with Subject B. Whichever gets more replies, use that for remaining 10.

**Example:**
- Subject A: `[Company] ready for NYC Local Law 144 audit?` (Direct)
- Subject B: `Can [Company] prove AI compliance if EEOC calls?` (Question-based)
