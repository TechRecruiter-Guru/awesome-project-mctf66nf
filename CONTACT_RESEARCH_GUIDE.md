## üéØ STEP-BY-STEP WORKFLOW (Per Company)

### Step 1: Find the Right Person on LinkedIn (2 minutes)

**Target Titles** (in order of priority):
1. **Chief People Officer** (CPO) - Best contact (executive decision maker)
2. **VP of People** / **VP of HR** - Also good (reports to CPO or CEO)
3. **General Counsel** / **Chief Legal Officer** - For compliance-focused pitch
4. **VP of Talent Acquisition** - If HR VP not findable
5. **Chief Compliance Officer** - For highly regulated industries (finance, healthcare)

**LinkedIn Search Process:**

#### Option A: LinkedIn Basic Search (Free)
1. Go to LinkedIn.com
2. In search bar, type: `VP HR [Company Name]`
   - Example: `VP HR Datadog`
3. Click "People" tab
4. Look for current employees with titles containing:
   - "Chief People Officer"
   - "VP of People"
   - "VP of Human Resources"
   - "General Counsel" (if HR not found)

#### Option B: LinkedIn Sales Navigator (Free Trial - More Powerful)
1. Sign up for Sales Navigator free trial: https://business.linkedin.com/sales-solutions/sales-navigator
2. Search ‚Üí Lead Search
3. Filters:
   - **Company:** Type company name (e.g., "Datadog")
   - **Job Title:** "Chief People Officer" OR "VP HR" OR "General Counsel"
   - **Seniority Level:** VP-level, C-level
4. Results will show exact matches with verified current employment

**What to Look For:**
- ‚úÖ Current employee (not past)
- ‚úÖ Title includes "People", "HR", "Talent", or "Legal"
- ‚úÖ Located in NYC/CA/IL (matches compliance jurisdiction)
- ‚úÖ Profile has recent activity (not dormant account)

**Red Flags:**
- ‚ùå "Recruiting Coordinator" (too junior, won't have budget)
- ‚ùå "HR Business Partner" (too tactical, not strategic)
- ‚ùå Past employee (left the company)

**Copy This Info to Your Spreadsheet:**

- **Location:** New York, NY (confirms LL144 compliance applies)

### Step 2: Find Their Email Using Hunter.io (1 minute)

#### Go to Hunter.io
1. Visit: https://hunter.io/
2. Sign up for free account (50 searches/month)
3. In search bar, type the **company domain**
   - Example: `datadog.com` (NOT "Datadog Inc")

#### Hunter.io Will Show You:
1. **Email Pattern** for the company
   - Example: `{first}.{last}@datadog.com`
   - Or: `{f}{last}@datadog.com`
   - Or: `{first}{last}@datadog.com`

2. **Confidence Score** (Hunter's algorithm predicts if email is valid)
   - 95%+ = Very likely correct
   - 70-94% = Probably correct (test before sending batch)
   - <70% = Risky (manually verify)

3. **Known Emails** (if Hunter has them in database)
   - Sometimes Hunter already knows the exact email for your target contact!

#### Construct the Email Manually:
If Hunter shows pattern but not the specific person:

**Pattern:** `{first}.{last}@datadog.com`
**Contact:** Sarah Johnson
**Email:** sarah.johnson@datadog.com

**Pattern:** `{f}{last}@datadog.com`

**Email:** sjohnson@datadog.com

**What to Look For:**

## ‚ö†Ô∏è TROUBLESHOOTING: What If You Can't Find the Contact?

### Problem 1: No VP HR on LinkedIn

**Solution:**
- Try alternative titles:
  - `Chief People Officer [Company]`
  - `Head of People [Company]`
  - `Director of HR [Company]`
  - `General Counsel [Company]` (legal angle)

### Problem 2: Multiple People with Same Title

**Solution:**
- Look for seniority indicators:
  - More connections (500+ is good)
  - Recent activity (posted in last 30 days)
  - Location matches compliance state (NYC/CA/IL)
- When in doubt, email the more senior person (CPO > VP > Director)

### Problem 3: Hunter.io Can't Find Email Pattern

**Solution:**
- Try Google: `email [Company Name] HR`
- Check company website: "Contact" or "Careers" page sometimes lists HR contact
- LinkedIn: Some people list email in their profile (rare but check)
- Last resort: Use generic HR email and ask for forwarding:
  - `hr@company.com`: "Hi, please forward to your VP of People or Compliance Officer."

### Problem 4: Email Bounces After Sending

**Solution:**
- Update spreadsheet: Mark as "Invalid email"
- Go back to Hunter.io, try alternative pattern
- Or find a different decision maker at same company
- Send LinkedIn message: "Hi [Name], I tried to email you at [email] but it bounced. What's the best way to reach you?"
