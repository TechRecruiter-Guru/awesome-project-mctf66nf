Operational Flight Envelope

Certified operational parameters and limitations

The operational flight envelope defines the boundaries within which the drone is certified to operate safely. All flight operations are continuously monitored and restricted to remain within these validated parameters.

Weather Limitations

✓

Precipitation Restrictions

No operations in moderate to heavy rain (>2.5mm/hr), snow, or hail. Light rain operations permitted with enhanced monitoring and reduced speed (10 m/s max).

✓

Visibility Requirements

Minimum 3 statute miles visibility required. Automated pre-flight weather check with real-time updates from AWOS/ASOS stations.

✓

Lightning & Thunderstorm Avoidance

No operations within 10 miles of active thunderstorms or lightning activity. Real-time integration with NOAA weather radar and lightning detection systems.

✓

Icing Conditions

No operations when icing conditions are forecast or observed. Temperature and humidity monitoring with automated mission abort if icing risk detected.

⚠️

Automatic Flight Envelope Protection

The flight control system continuously monitors all operational parameters and automatically restricts flight to remain within certified envelope. If conditions exceed safe limits, the system initiates return-to-home or controlled landing sequence.

Detect and Avoid Systems

Multi-layered airspace awareness and collision avoidance

Strategic Deconfliction (Cooperative)

✓

UTM (UAS Traffic Management) Integration

Real-time flight intent sharing with FAA-approved UTM Service Supplier (USS). Automatic 4D trajectory coordination with other UTM participants. Conflict resolution 5 minutes in advance.

✓

Remote ID (ASTM F3411 Compliant)

Broadcast Remote ID transmitting position, altitude, velocity, and operator location. Standard Remote ID module with both broadcast and network transmission.

✓

ADS-B In Receiver

1090MHz ADS-B receiver for detection of transponder-equipped aircraft. Real-time tracking of cooperative manned aircraft within 20+ mile radius. Automatic avoidance maneuvers triggered at 1-mile separation.

✓

NOTAMs & TFR Awareness

Automated pre-flight and in-flight NOTAM checking. Real-time TFR database updates with geofencing to prevent entry into restricted airspace. Stadium and emergency TFR auto-detection.

Tactical Deconfliction (Non-Cooperative)

✓

Electro-Optical/Infrared (EO/IR) Cameras

Multi-spectral camera system with AI-based aircraft detection. 360° coverage through 6 wide-angle cameras. Detection range: 1.5km for small aircraft, 3km for large aircraft. Automatic tracking and avoidance.

✓

Acoustic Aircraft Detection

Microphone array for detection of non-cooperative aircraft by engine/rotor noise. Effective for helicopters and small aircraft within 500m. Provides directional cues for camera system targeting.

✓

Ground-Based Radar Surveillance

Supplementary ground radar network provides airspace surveillance along delivery routes. 5km range per station. Data shared via LTE link to provide extended awareness beyond onboard sensor range.

✓

Collision Avoidance Logic

Automated maneuver selection prioritizing vertical avoidance (descent). Minimum horizontal separation: 500 feet. Minimum vertical separation: 100 feet. Right-of-way rules programmed per 14 CFR Part 107.

✓

Detect and Avoid Performance

Over 25,000 autonomous flights with zero near-mid-air collisions (NMAC). Detect and avoid system has executed 47 avoidance maneuvers for aircraft conflicts, all resolved at >1,000 feet horizontal separation.
